## Economy
Driven by five key industries:
- maritime trade and shipping: trade hub
- agriculture:
	- maize, wheat, barley, sugar, beets, peaches, tomatoes, cotton, almonds, grapes, melons, potatoes and citrus fruits
	- olives (especially for olive oil)
	- luxury goods such as tabbacco and wine 
- textiles 
- goods, such as fine pottery, glassware, jewellery and high quality dyes; cypress timber and other goods for shipbuilding
- selling and reselling of arcane goods, such as special inks, potion ingredients and other arcane artefacts 

## Demographics
Entodiff’s population is largely made up of humans. However, due to the access to the sea, many travelers have made Entodiff their home.

Next to humans, halflings make up the largest part of the population at around 10%. 

Most of the elves who can be found within the city, are the amphibious coastal dwelling sea elves. Most sea elves who live within Entodiff can be found in the south by the coastline. Their homes are close to the ocean, and some of their buildings are also built to reach into the ocean itself. 

Even a very small number of merfolk live amongst the sea elves in Entodiff. 

Tieflings in Entodiff mostly live in ghettos, as tieflings are widely distrusted. They are often stereotyped as petty criminals, manipulators and as dangerous. As decent jobs are difficult to come by for tieflings, many tend to fall into these stereotypes for survival.  

Orcs, half-orcs, any goblinoids and descendants of drow and drow themselves are treated in very similar fashion to tieflings. 

Drow and half-drow men are seen as week and meak, having their masculinity ridiculed. The women are seen as brutish.
