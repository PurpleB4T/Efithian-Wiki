# Xenia Iridaceae Menu


## **Appetizers and Sides:**
- **_Kalamrakia tiganita -_** squid rings and tentacles drenched in flour and seasoned, a fried until crispy and golden brown. Garnished with freshly chopped parsley and complemented by lemon wedges.
- **_Feta Salad_** - made from cucumber, olives, tomatoes and feta cheese
- **_Bread and Olive Oil_** - bread of your choice served with olive oil
- **_Stuffed Grapevine Leaves_** - stuffed with lemon flavored rice, fresh herbs and spices.
- **_Warm Pita Triangles_**
- **_Warm pretzels_** - with or without salt. Fresh from the oven.
- **_Halfling style red herring salad_** - pickled red herring, apples, onions, beets and pickles in a rich creamy sauce. Served with toasted slices of bread.

## **Main Dishes:**
- **_Moussaka_** - Spiced lamb or beef cooked in a rich tomato sauce, then layered with fried eggplant and béchamel sauce and baked til the flavors have all seeped through.  Creamy, delicious, and fit for a balmy summer evening under the stars.
- **_Papoutsakia_** _-_ Eggplants stuffed with a tomato-based lamb sauce, topped with a creamy béchamel sauce and sprinkled with cheese, it is then baked til it comes out golden.  
    Comes with a crisp fresh salad and your bread of choice.
- **_Bakaliriaros_** - Crispy cod fritters, with a thick garlicy dip and served with rice and vegetables.
- **_Grilled Octopus_** - Marinated in olive oil, vinegar, red wine and oregano. Served with fresh salad and rice.
- **_Astakomakaronada_** - Lobster meat coupled with a flavorful tomato-based sauce served over pasta. Enriched with wine. Lobster meat served in shells and garnished with parsley.
- **_Garides saganaki_** - sautéed shrimps, doused in a rich tomato sauce and topped with crumbled feta cheese. Served with warm pita triangles.
- **_Mydia_** - fresh muscles steamed in red wine, fresh herbs, garlic and a variety of garden vegetables. Doused in a red sauce.

## **Deserts:**
- **_Galaktoboureko_** - custard pie, soaked with a cold spiced syrup. Served both hot and cold
- **_Halva_** - small confectionery made with semolina and olive oil. Spiced with cinnamon, lemon and orange peel.
- **_Orange Pie_**
- **_Apfelkuchen_** - a halfling style apple pie.
- **_Käsekuchen_** - a halfling style cheesecake.

## **Breakfast Options**
- **_Scrambled Eggs_** - diced tomatos with beaten eggs, cooked until fluffy. Topped with crumbled with feta and either served with the bread of your choice or warm pita triangles.
- **_Cheese Pie with nuts and honey_** - cheese pancakes, served with honey and roasted nuts.
- **_Flatbread with Tomatos_** - flatbread, topped with tomatos, onions and drizzled in onion oil; baked until crisp.
- **_Brotzeit_** - halfling style breakfast platter, with bread of your choice, a cheese platter, a meat platter and a hard boiled egg.
