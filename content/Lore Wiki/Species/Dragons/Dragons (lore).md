# **Dragons (lore)**
> [!info|bg-c-purple]- Overview
> **Dragons** (or **wyrms**) were very powerful and magical creatures. There were several types of dragons, the most common of which were [[Lore Wiki/Species/Dragons/Chromatic Dragons\|chromatic]] and [[Lore Wiki/Species/Dragons/Metallic Dragons\|metallic]], which were evil and good respectively. They were an ancient race. Few species that still exist can claim longer lineage.
